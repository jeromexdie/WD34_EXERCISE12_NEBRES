SAGE, THE RADIANT HEALER

Originating from the Shaanxi province in China, Sage is a Radiant healer whose capacity to turn the tide of battle is immeasurable. She is the seventh Agent to join the VALORANT PROTOCOL. Sage is a Geomancer, someone with the power to manipulate the Earth. She channels her abilities through a set of crystalline Radianite orbs, which she carries on her belt. Using these, Sage is able to create physical structures in the world. Part of Sage's kit also involves orbs made of pure radianite crystals that, while not an aspect of her power, are rather tools she's made for herself to make use of.

Sage has a kind and compassionate personality, being referred to by some as almost 'mother-like'. While still pretty young, Sage exudes an inner wisdom of someone much older and more mature[1]. She genuinely cares about the wellbeing of her allies, and places great emphasis on collaboration and combined victories.

As suggested by the DIE FOR YOU Music Video, Sage's primary character flaw stems from this caring nature: Sage feels a compulsion to spread her healing to everyone she can; she considers it her duty to solve all the problems and ailments of those she holds close. This insistence on protecting her team forms a 'fatal flaw' of sorts. One of the best in-game representations of this is her voiceline interaction with Viper.
