Valorant is a free-to-play first-person tactical hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows.

Initiatior
	- Initiators challenge angles by setting up their team to enter contested ground and push defenders away.
	- KAY/O, SOVA, SKYE, FADE, BREACH 

Sentinel
	- Sentinel are defensive experts who can lock down areas and watch flanks, both on attaker and defender rounds.
	- CHAMBER, CYPHER, KILLJOY, SAGE

Duelist
	- Duelists are self-sufficient fraggers who their team expects, through abilities and skills, to get high-frags and seek out engagements first.
	- JETT, NEON, PHOENIX, RAZE, REYNA, YORU

Controller
	- Controllers are experts in slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success.
	- ASTRA, BRIM, VIPER, OMEN, HARBOR
